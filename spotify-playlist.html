This app talks to Spotify directly from your browser — nothing is sent to any other server. Because of that, it needs your own free Spotify app credentials:

1. Open the Spotify Developer Dashboard and create an app (any name works).
2. In its settings, add the exact Redirect URI below, then save.
3. Copy the Client ID shown on the app page and paste it here.
