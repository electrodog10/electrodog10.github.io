RV Park WiFi Service Solution

Description

  • Installed successfully at four parks

  • August 2021 - Present

After my RV Repair Club video was published, my company received an email from an RV park owner in Colorado inquiring about WiFi services. This soon led to a successful engagement and a realization that if someone is willing to seeking out Callaway Technologies for RV Park WiFi solutions based on a video, then even a small amount of promotion could lead to more clients. I revamped the RV Park WiFi section of my website and set up a small budget on Google Ads for a test run. This led to four leads and two more clients in Washington and Indiana – a 50% conversion rate! This project was contributory to my future goals – the ability to take a specialized installation I did for one park and turn it into a product I can successfully market was something I knew I wanted to do on a larger scale.
