CPA Office Build Out

Description

  • Installed successfully at four parks

  • August 2021 - Present

My company pitched our proposal to a CPA firm to coordinate the technological portion of their move to a new office. Since the new space was not previously an office space, it was completely remodeled. Callaway Technologies determined the requirements, coordinated low-voltage wiring installation, and performed A/V integration while the office was being readied. When the office was finished, I moved the critical infrastructure to the new office but had a narrow time frame of a few hours because of it being tax season. There were only two to three hours of downtime during office hours which was a success.

Skills Utilized

  • IP Networking
  • Low Voltage Cabling
  • Vendor Coordination
  • Effective Client Communication
